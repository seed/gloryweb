ABOUT

Glory is a Sydney-based association that helps to run local skateboarding events for the community. Our aim is to provide aspiration to the youth while encouraging respect, discipline and fun.

Going to the skatepark on your own can be intimidating. Tell us about it! At Glory we organise group skate sessions, so you don't get that daunting feeling when you hit your favourite spot.

Every once in a while we throw a BBQ by a local skatepark. Always a good vibe! Find us at Waterloo's Fernside, Redfern's Russel Crowe, Sydenham's Green or South Eveleigh's ATP skatepark. Events get announce on this page, so stay tuned.

You are new to skateboarding? We have highly skilled stuntmen who'd be more than happy to guide you through landing your first kickflip.
